Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Tugawe Feedback. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services. By using Tugawe Feedback, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Data Collection

We collect two main types of data: personal information and usage data. Personal information includes details like your name, email address, and phone number, which you provide when signing up or using our services. Usage data includes information about how you interact with our platform, such as the feedback you collect and the settings you configure.

3. Data Usage

The data we collect is used to deliver feedback to your business, analyze customer responses, and ensure the security of our platform. We may also use your information to communicate with you about updates, new features, and support.

4. Data Storage and Protection

We store your data securely using encryption and limit access to authorized personnel only. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, delete, or withdraw your consent for the use of your data. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except when necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Any third-party services we use are bound by their own privacy policies.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. These tools help us understand how you use our services and improve their functionality. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically. Your continued use of Tugawe Feedback after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@tugawefeedback.com
